Project 3

ArchDeco

A platform for booking brow appointments

Project Overview

The Arch Deco website is a platform dedicated to showcasing the services and expertise of a permanent brow business. The business specializes in providing high-quality, long-lasting eyebrow enhancements and beauty solutions.

The website serves as a virtual storefront for the business, allowing visitors to explore the various services offered, learn about the brow artists, and access important information such as pricing, booking options, and contact details.

In addition to service information, the website also provides valuable resources and educational content related to eyebrow care and maintenance. Visitors can learn about proper eyebrow grooming techniques, aftercare instructions, and tips for maintaining the longevity of their brow enhancements.

The Arch Deco website is built using modern web technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It incorporates responsive design principles to ensure a seamless browsing experience across different devices and screen sizes. The website also utilizes SEO best practices to improve visibility in search engine results, helping to attract potential clients and grow the business's online presence.
